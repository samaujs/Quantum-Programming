# Learn Quantum Computation using Qiskit
The repository contains some of the examples covered in the IBM Quantum Computing Workshop (18 - 20 May 2020).

- Explore the basic constructions of the Quantum Circuits using Qiskit.
- Explore Aer with QasmSimulator and backends, including the introduction of noises to the Quantum Circuits.
- Explore the combinations and decompositions of Quantum Circuits together with visualizations and snaphots.
- Explore the pulses, schedule and transpile on Quantum Circuits with 'ibmq_armonk' backend.
